Story:

RRR is a fictional story inspired by the lives of two Indian freedom fighters, Alluri Sitarama Raju and Komaram Bheem. Set during British rule in the 1920s, the film follows their journey from strangers to close friends as they fight against injustice, oppression, and colonial rule. The movie is known for its spectacular action, emotional storytelling, and visual effects.
